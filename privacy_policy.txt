 Privacy Policy for Mendalia AI

  Effective Date: April 14, 2026
  Last Updated: April 14, 2026

  Mendalia AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to
  protecting the privacy and security of clinical and
  personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we
  collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when
  you use the Mendalia AI mobile application and web
  platform (the "Service").

  Mendalia AI is a clinical reasoning support tool
  designed for healthcare professionals. By using the
  Service, you agree to the collection and use of
  information in accordance with this policy.

  ---

  1. Information We Collect

  A. Personal Account Information
  When you register for an account, we collect:
   * Identity Data: Full name, professional title, and
     gender (for avatar customization).
   * Contact Data: Email address and phone number (for
     OTP verification).
   * Authentication Data: Google Sign-In identifiers and
     Firebase Auth tokens.

  B. Clinical & Patient Data (The "Processed Data")
  As an AI-native ambient reasoning engine, we process:
   * Audio Recordings: Ambient recordings of clinical
     consultations (collected only with clinician
     activation).
   * Transcriptions: Text-based versions of the audio
     recordings.
   * Medical Documents: Photos or PDFs of lab results,
     reports, and clinical notes uploaded by the user.
   * Patient Metadata: Names or identifiers used to
     organize cases within the app.

  C. Automatically Collected Data
   * Usage Data: Information on how the Service is
     accessed (e.g., feature usage, session duration).
   * Device Information: IP address, device type,
     operating system version, and unique device
     identifiers.

  ---

  2. How We Use Your Information

  We use the collected data for the following purposes:
   * AI Reasoning: To generate differential diagnoses,
     care plans, and clinical letters using Large
     Language Models (LLMs).
   * Service Delivery: To maintain your account, sync
     cases across devices, and provide real-time updates
     via Firestore.
   * Communication: To send OTP codes, security alerts,
     and support responses.
   * Improvement: To monitor usage patterns and improve
     the accuracy of our clinical reasoning algorithms.

  ---

  3. Data Processing and Storage

  A. Single Source of Truth
  All real-time analysis data is stored in Google Cloud
  Firestore. We employ a "Patching Strategy" to ensure
  data integrity and real-time synchronization between
  our Django backend and your mobile/web client.

  B. AI Processing
  Clinical data (transcripts and reports) is processed
  via secure Google Cloud Functions and Vertex AI.
   * Data Isolation: We do not use your specific patient
     data to train global, multi-tenant Large Language
     Models.
   * Ephemeral Processing: Audio recordings are
     processed for transcription and reasoning, then
     handled according to our data retention schedule.

  C. Security Measures
  We implement industry-standard security protocols,
  including:
   * Encryption in Transit: All data is sent via
     HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
   * Encryption at Rest: Data in Firestore and GCS is
     encrypted at the disk level.
   * Authentication: Secure JWT (JSON Web Tokens) and
     Firebase Authentication.

  ---

  4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  We do not sell your personal or clinical data. We
  share data only in the following circumstances:
   * Service Providers: With trusted third parties like
     Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for hosting and AI
     processing.
   * Legal Requirements: If required by law to comply
     with a subpoena or similar legal process.
   * Safety: When we believe disclosure is necessary to
     protect our rights or the safety of users.

  ---

  5. Clinician Responsibility & Patient Consent

  Mendalia AI is a tool for healthcare professionals.
   * Consent: It is the sole responsibility of the
     clinician to obtain the necessary verbal or written
     consent from patients before using the ambient
     recording or document upload features.
   * De-identification: While our AI assists in
     reasoning, clinicians are encouraged to follow best
     practices for data minimization.

  ---

  6. Data Retention and Deletion

  We retain account information as long as your account
  is active. You may request the deletion of specific
  cases or your entire account at any time through the
  app settings or by contacting connect@mendalia.com.
  Upon account deletion, all associated patient data is
  purged from our active databases.

  ---

  7. International Data Transfers

  Your information may be transferred to—and maintained
  on—computers located outside of your state or country
  where data protection laws may differ. Our primary
  servers are located in the India and USA via Google
  Cloud Platform.

  ---

  8. Your Rights

  Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
   * Access the personal data we hold about you.
   * Request correction of inaccurate data.
   * Object to the processing of your data.
   * Request the portability of your data.

  ---

  If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or
  our data practices, please contact our support team:
